About Me

With over 7 years in the digital marketing and design field, I’ve worked across New Zealand’s retail landscape, including brands like Huffer and Sunglass Style. My focus is crafting marketing strategies for local businesses that drive results while pairing them with engaging content and storytelling. I’m into working with good people who enjoy what they do.

After a life-changing two years as a stay-at-home dad, I’m now freelancing from Devonport, Auckland, with a fresh perspective and sharpened design instincts from carefully watching the digital landscape change by the day. I believe the ability to authentically connect with your audience is what truly cuts through.

I’m also the founder of @milesrunclub , a platform that explores the intersection of running culture and style, and New Zealand’s leading niche account in this space. This is paired with a custom-built e-commerce store, selling casual running wear in limited releases to a worldwide community of followers.
